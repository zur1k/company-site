challenge

Richard came up with an idea to develop an enterprise level system for attorneys which main goal was to collect information from clients and complete physical isolation of data for different cases and management of data access levels.

The task is non-standard from the technical side because it is necessary to dynamically create new databases, economically manage them and monitor new DBMS.
